📱 Polycam Workflow

  1. Open Polycam → scan your room in Space or LiDAR mode
  2. Tap Export → GLTF — it will download a zip file
  3. AirDrop / email the file to your computer
  4. Click Choose Polycam File and select the .zip directly — do not unzip it
  5. Room dimensions auto-populate from the scan ✓
